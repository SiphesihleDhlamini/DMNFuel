About DMN Fuel

Our Story

Founded in 1995, DMN Fuel began as a small family-owned business with a single delivery truck and a commitment to exceptional service. Over nearly three decades, we've grown into one of the region's most trusted diesel fuel suppliers, serving Southern Africa with excellence.

Mission Statement

To build long term relationships and be the preferred fuel partner of choice to our clients. Our purpose is to assist in RSA's energy requirements guided by relentless focus on our identified business imperatives. Honesty, integrity and commitment are vital to driving our growth and success. We maintain a high standard of business practices which translate into unparalleled value for our shareholders.

To achieve our mission, we conduct our business with six key responsibilities in mind:

  • Obey the law
  • Stimulate economic growth
  • Be environmentally responsible
  • Expand opportunities for our employees
  • Respect our vendors
  • Reward our shareholders

Vision Statement

To be pioneering leaders in the provision of fuel in Southern Africa, driven by strategic partnerships. Our vision is to be the benchmark of emerging oil companies by creating and maintaining a sustainable competitive advantage through service excellence. In dealing with our customers, we value long term business relationships, as well as try and accommodate the varying customer needs, based on the industry they operate in. We place great emphasis on close liaison with clients in order to adequately meet client individual needs.

Key Values

At DMN Fuel we:

  • Put trust first - Building relationships based on reliability and integrity
  • Are honest - Transparency in all our business dealings
  • Have highest reliability - We have the capacity to deliver when you need us
  • Have high efficiency - Promptness in service delivery every time
  • Are innovative - We strive to understand client processes and use innovative methods to solve problems and add value to your organization

Our Commitment to You

We will do everything to directly benefit you. Our medium term goal is to develop strategic partnership with stakeholders, clients and other service providers that lead to long term relationships. This will help us build trust, maximize our service offerings and continue to grow together.

To benefit you in a long term, we are in a process of building our own strategic located storage terminals and our fleet of tankers to reduce the turnaround time in fuel supply and have total control of our distribution mechanism that will help lower the cost and value to our customers.
